# Maintenance Procedures

## Replacing the AHU-12 Supply Fan Belt
1. Lock out and tag out the supply fan motor.
2. Loosen the motor base adjustment bolts.
3. Slide the motor to release belt tension and remove the old belt.
4. Fit the new belt and adjust tension to 10 millimetres deflection at mid-span.
5. Tighten the fan bearing housing bolts to 30 newton metres.
6. Remove the lockout and run the fan to confirm normal speed.

## Quarterly Chiller CH-02 Inspection
1. Record chilled water supply and condenser water temperatures.
2. Check oil pressure is within 200 to 250 kilopascals.
3. Inspect for refrigerant leaks at the compressor and flanges.
4. Confirm approach temperature stays below 1.5 degrees Celsius.
5. Log readings and raise a work order for any value outside range.

## Cleaning Condenser Tubes
1. Isolate the condenser water side and drain.
2. Brush each tube with a nylon brush and flush with clean water.
3. Inspect for scaling and pitting.
4. Refill, vent air, and confirm condenser pressure returns to normal range.

## Checking Refrigerant Charge
1. Connect gauges to the service ports.
2. Compare evaporator and condenser pressures against the spec ranges.
3. If pressures indicate undercharge, leak test before adding refrigerant.
4. Record the charge weight added on the work order.
