# Equipment Spec — CH-02 (Water-Cooled Chiller)

## Overview
CH-02 is a water-cooled centrifugal chiller in the basement plant room. Manufacturer: York, model YK. Installed 2018. It is the lead chiller for the building cooling loop.

## Key Specifications
- Cooling capacity: 500 tons nominal.
- Refrigerant: R-134a, charge 180 kilograms.
- Compressor: single-stage centrifugal, 315 kW.
- Condenser water flow: 1,500 gallons per minute.

## Normal Operating Ranges
- Chilled water supply temperature: 6 to 7 degrees Celsius.
- Condenser water supply temperature: 29 to 32 degrees Celsius.
- Oil pressure: 200 to 250 kilopascals.
- Approach temperature: below 1.5 degrees Celsius.

## Safety Cutouts
- High condenser pressure trip: 1,200 kilopascals.
- Low evaporator pressure trip: 250 kilopascals.
- Low oil pressure trip: 140 kilopascals.
