# Fault Code Reference

## F-203 — Low Refrigerant Pressure
Indicates evaporator pressure below the safe threshold. Common causes: refrigerant leak, blocked expansion valve, or low charge. Action: isolate the unit, check for leaks with an electronic detector, and escalate if charge is below 90 percent. Severity is usually high.

## F-117 — High Condenser Pressure
Indicates condenser pressure approaching the trip limit. Common causes: dirty condenser tubes, low condenser water flow, or air in the loop. Action: verify condenser water flow and clean tubes. Severity is medium to high.

## F-088 — Supply Fan Belt Slip
Indicates the supply fan is not reaching commanded speed. Common causes: worn or loose belt. Action: inspect and re-tension or replace the belt. Severity is low to medium.

## F-301 — Low Oil Pressure
Indicates compressor oil pressure below the trip limit. Common causes: oil pump wear, clogged oil filter, or low oil level. Action: stop the compressor and inspect the oil system. Severity is critical.

## F-145 — Dirty Filter
Indicates filter pressure drop above the alarm threshold. Action: replace the panel filters. Severity is low.
