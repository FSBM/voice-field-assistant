# Equipment Spec — AHU-12 (Air Handling Unit)

## Overview
AHU-12 is a roof-mounted air handling unit serving Plant Room B. Manufacturer: Carrier, model 39M. Installed 2019. It conditions supply air for floors 3 to 5.

## Key Specifications
- Supply airflow: 8,000 CFM nominal.
- Cooling coil: 6-row chilled water coil, design entering water temperature 6 degrees Celsius.
- Supply fan motor: 7.5 kW, 400 V three-phase, belt driven.
- Filter bank: MERV 13 panel filters, replaced every 90 days.

## Torque Specifications
- Compressor mounting bolts: 45 newton metres.
- Fan bearing housing bolts: 30 newton metres.
- Coil header flange bolts: 25 newton metres.

## Normal Operating Ranges
- Supply air temperature: 13 to 15 degrees Celsius in cooling mode.
- Chilled water differential pressure: 50 to 70 kilopascals.
- Filter pressure drop alarm threshold: 250 pascals.
